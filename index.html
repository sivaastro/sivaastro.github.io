Who am i ?

I am a Ph.D. candidate and NASA Miro scholar in the SpaceTREx laboratory at the University of Arizona. I am leading a team with 10 members working on Lunar Development. We are developing end-to-end autonomous robotic systems to assist in lunar construction, mining, and lunar industry development with NASA MIRO, UREP, and M-STAR funding. We closely collaborate with MDA Space and NASA JPL on space robotics development.

MISSION

As a Space Robotic researcher, I aim to make our home planet a better place to live by sustainably exploring and extracting resources from our solar system. By shifting towards space mining, we aim to reduce the environmental impact and pollution associated with traditional Earth-based mining practices, offering a more responsible and eco-friendly approach to resource extraction to advance human civilization.
